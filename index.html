Chicago Civic Hack Night

Join us every Tuesday to build, learn and share.

Join us next Tuesday

Come join a group of passionate folks working at the intersection of open government, cities, and technology. This will be an evening of civic tech hacking, collaboration, learning and networking.

This event, organized by civic tech group Open City, is for those who want to:

  • get work done on open gov projects
  • start a new project, or find one to join (non-coders very welcome!)
  • bounce ideas and get help from tech and gov experts
  • learn about open data, smart cities, and open gov

There will be food!

Special thanks to DataMade for hosting us at 1871!

Sessions

Civic Hacking 101 Session

New to civic hacking? Don't even know what that means? Every week, Christopher Whitaker leads a 15 minute breakout session that will get you up to speed. You can also watch the online version.

Monthly Data Potluck

Once a month, we partner with Data Potluck and put on a joint Hack Night & Potluck with data science presentations. We encourage civic hackers to come meet and partner with members of this talented group!

Bring the hack night to your city

The idea of a weekly civic hacking event has spread to cities like:

  • London
  • New York
  • San Francisco
  • Oakland
  • Boston
  • Philadelphia
  • Washington DC

It acts as an anchor to build up the volunteer civic hacking community while avoiding 'hackathon fatigue' and has been crutial to the success we've seen in Chicago. Check out the Code for America blog to learn how to organize a hack night in your city.
