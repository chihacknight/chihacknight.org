Other Chicago civic technology events

Check out the ever-growing ecosystem of civic tech events in Chicago.

  • Open Gov Chicago(-land) Meetup - The group that started it all in Chicago. This meetup is for citizens who are interested in seeing their federal, state, and local government function more efficiently and responsively.

  • The Chicago Data Visualization Group - This is a group of data visualization enthusiasts interested in networking, learning, professional development, and competing in visualization contests.

  • Chicago City Data User Group - You don't have to be a developer to use Chicago's rich sets of data! The Chicago City Data users group is for end users, business users, enthusiasts, students, entrepreneurs, anyone interested in how to use City Data.
